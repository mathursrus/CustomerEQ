## MVP Scope

### Hero Feature: Real-Time Feedback-to-Action Engine
**Core Capability:** AI-powered system that automatically routes customer feedback to appropriate loyalty actions within 15 minutes of submission.

**Value Delivery:**
- Reduces feedback-to-action time from weeks to minutes
- Increases customer retention through immediate response
- Eliminates manual workflow management overhead
- Provides measurable ROI through closed-loop optimization

**Technical Approach:**
- Event-driven architecture with real-time processing
- AI/ML models for sentiment analysis and decision-making
- Rule-based workflow engine with machine learning optimization
- API-first design for extensibility and integrations

### Supporting Scope (8-Month MVP)
1. **Basic Feedback Collection & Sentiment Analysis** (3-4 weeks)
2. **Simple Customer Profile Management** (2-3 weeks)
3. **Basic Loyalty Action Triggers** (4-5 weeks)
4. **Real-Time Processing Pipeline** (5-6 weeks)
5. **Basic Analytics Dashboard** (2-3 weeks)
6. **Essential CRM Integrations** (3-4 weeks) - Salesforce, HubSpot

### Excluded From MVP
- Advanced AI personalization (complex ML models)
- Multi-channel communication (multiple API integrations)
- Enterprise features (multi-brand, advanced security)
- Sophisticated A/B testing (statistical analysis complexity)
- Custom workflow builder (complex UI/UX requirements)

### First Validation Test: Concierge MVP
**Phase 1 (Months 1-2):** Manual execution of automated workflows for 3-5 pilot customers
- Human-powered sentiment analysis and customer profiling
- Manual loyalty action deployment within 2-4 hours
- Spreadsheet-based tracking and reporting
- Validate customer value before technical investment

**Success Criteria:**
- 3+ customers complete 60-day concierge pilot
- Average customer satisfaction improvement >10%
- Customers express willingness to pay for automated solution
- Clear workflow patterns emerge for automation

## Risks and Mitigations

### Critical Risks

**Risk: Technical Execution Complexity (70% probability)**
Real-time processing and AI orchestration proves more complex than anticipated, delaying market entry and increasing development costs.
**Mitigation:** Start with near-real-time processing (5-15 minutes), partner with proven infrastructure providers, build technical advisory board with relevant expertise.

**Risk: Feature Parity Trap (70% probability)**  
Attempting to match every feature of specialized platforms dilutes core value proposition and extends development timeline.
**Mitigation:** Maintain "Integration Killer" positioning focus, develop clear "not our job" framework, create integration value calculator to shift conversations from features to ROI.

**Risk: Competitive Response Speed (60% probability)**
Incumbents respond faster than expected through acquisitions or native feature development, compressing market window.
**Mitigation:** Rapid market entry through MVV approach, establish customer relationships and switching costs, maintain technical differentiation through continuous innovation.

**Risk: Organizational Change Resistance (40% probability)**
CX and loyalty teams resist unified platform due to territorial concerns, slowing adoption and customer success.
**Mitigation:** Position as "team empowerment" rather than consolidation, develop role-specific value propositions, implement gradual migration paths.

### Manageable Risks

**Risk: Market Timing Shifts (30% probability)**
Economic conditions or technology trends change market demand patterns.
**Mitigation:** Flexible go-to-market strategy, multiple customer segments, value-based pricing models.

**Risk: Funding Requirements Exceed Projections (35% probability)**
Development complexity or market entry costs higher than estimated.
**Mitigation:** Phased development approach, early revenue generation through concierge model, strategic partnerships to reduce costs.
