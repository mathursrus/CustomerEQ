## Description (≤4000 chars)

```
CustomerEQ puts your customer experience data in your pocket.

CustomerEQ is a B2B CX and loyalty platform (customereq.wellnessatwork.me) used by mid-market businesses to collect NPS surveys, monitor Google Reviews, and turn customer signals into loyalty program actions in under 15 minutes.

This companion app gives CustomerEQ account holders — typically CX managers, operations leads, and business owners — instant mobile access to the same insights available on the web dashboard.

WHAT YOU CAN SEE
• Dashboard — Live NPS score, response count, and review rating for your organization
• Reviews — Your Google Review feed with sentiment indicators; reply from the app
• Surveys — All active and closed NPS/CSAT surveys; drill into individual responses and trend charts
• Insights — AI-generated topic clusters from open-text feedback and anomaly alerts when response patterns shift

SWITCHING BETWEEN BUSINESSES
If you manage multiple locations or brands under one account, tap the org switcher on your Profile tab to move between them — all data refreshes automatically.

ABOUT THE DATA
All data is your own — sourced from your CustomerEQ account. The app never accesses your contacts, camera, microphone, location, or health data. Authentication is handled by Clerk. AI-generated insights are produced by the CustomerEQ platform and are informational.

ACCOUNT REQUIRED
CustomerEQ is a paid B2B SaaS. This app requires an active CustomerEQ account at customereq.wellnessatwork.me. It cannot function without one. Contact support@wellnessatwork.me to request access.
```

## App Review Notes (for Apple Review team)

> CustomerEQ is the mobile companion to a paid B2B CX-loyalty SaaS (customereq.wellnessatwork.me). The app surfaces the account holder's own data — their survey responses, Google Review feed, NPS scores, and AI-generated feedback clusters — from the CustomerEQ backend. It cannot function without an active CustomerEQ account. The parent platform serves mid-market businesses in the wellness and hospitality verticals. This is the first version of the iOS app; authentication uses Clerk (clerk.customereq.wellnessatwork.me). A demo account can be provided to the review team on request.
